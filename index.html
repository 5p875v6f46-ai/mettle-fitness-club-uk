About the Club

You don't have to do this alone.

Mettle Fitness Club UK is an outdoor fitness and wellness community designed for women in perimenopause, menopause and beyond.

We focus on building strength, boosting energy and supporting hormonal health in a safe, supportive and encouraging environment — where every woman is celebrated at every stage of her journey.

Questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I need to be fit to join?

+

Absolutely not. Mettle is designed for women at every fitness level. Our coaches will tailor every session to meet you where you are and progress at a pace that works for your body.

What does 'outdoor' training mean?

+

We train in beautiful outdoor locations around Solihull. Fresh air and natural settings are a core part of the Mettle experience — proven to improve mood, reduce stress and boost motivation.

Is this suitable for menopause symptoms?

+

Yes — our hormone-smart approach is specifically designed to support women through perimenopause and menopause. Training in the right way can significantly ease symptoms and improve quality of life.

What is the Founding Members offer?

+

Founding members get a special discounted price that will never be offered again — in exchange for a 6-week commitment and helping shape what Mettle becomes. Only 20 places are available.

Where are sessions held?

+

Sessions are held in and around Solihull, UK. Exact locations are shared with members upon joining. We always choose accessible, welcoming outdoor spots.

What do I need to bring?

+

Just yourself, comfortable outdoor clothing and a water bottle. We provide all equipment. No experience required — just an open mind and the willingness to show up for yourself.
